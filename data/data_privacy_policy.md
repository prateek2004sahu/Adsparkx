# Data Privacy & Security Policy

## Data We Collect
- Account information (name, email, company)
- Usage data (features used, login times, session duration)
- Campaign performance data
- Payment information (processed by Stripe; we do not store card numbers)
- API request logs (retained for 90 days)

## How We Use Your Data
- Deliver and improve our services
- Send transactional emails (invoices, alerts)
- Marketing communications (opt-out available)
- Fraud detection and security monitoring
- Aggregate analytics (anonymized)

## Data Retention
| Data Type         | Retention Period         |
|-------------------|--------------------------|
| Account data      | Duration of account + 30 days post-cancellation |
| Campaign data     | 2 years from campaign end |
| API logs          | 90 days                  |
| Billing records   | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Support tickets   | 3 years                  |

## GDPR Compliance (EU Users)
- Legal basis: Contract performance and legitimate interest
- Data Processing Agreement available upon request
- EU data stored in Frankfurt, Germany (AWS eu-central-1)
- Right to access, rectify, erase, and port your data

### Exercising Your Rights
Email privacy@adsparkx.com with subject "Data Rights Request" and include:
- Full name
- Account email
- Type of request (access / deletion / portability / correction)
- We respond within 30 days

## CCPA Compliance (California Users)
- We do not sell personal data
- California residents may request deletion of personal data
- Contact privacy@adsparkx.com to exercise CCPA rights

## Data Security Measures
- All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- SOC 2 Type II certified (report available on request)
- Penetration tested annually by third-party security firm
- Multi-factor authentication required for admin access
- Employee access to customer data is role-based and logged

## Data Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach affecting your account:
- We will notify you within 72 hours per GDPR requirements
- Notification sent to registered account email
- We will provide details: nature of breach, data involved, steps taken
