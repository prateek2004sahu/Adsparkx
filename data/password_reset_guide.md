# Password Reset & Account Access Troubleshooting

## How to Reset Your Password

### Standard Password Reset
1. Go to app.adsparkx.com/login
2. Click "Forgot Password?" below the login form
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your inbox for a reset email (may take up to 5 minutes)
5. Click the link in the email — it expires in 30 minutes
6. Enter and confirm your new password (min 8 characters, 1 uppercase, 1 number)

### If You Don't Receive the Reset Email
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Ensure you're using the email address linked to your account
- Add noreply@adsparkx.com to your safe senders list
- Wait 5 minutes and try again
- If still not received, contact support

## Account Lockout

### Why Was My Account Locked?
Accounts are locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts for security purposes. The lockout lasts 15 minutes automatically.

### How to Unlock Your Account
**Option 1 (Automatic):** Wait 15 minutes and try again

**Option 2 (Immediate):** 
1. Go to app.adsparkx.com/unlock
2. Enter your email address
3. Verify identity via the link sent to your email

**Option 3 (Support):** Contact our support team with your account email and user ID

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Issues

### Lost Access to Authenticator App
1. Use one of your 10 backup codes (provided during 2FA setup)
2. If backup codes are lost, contact support with:
   - Government ID
   - Account email
   - Last 4 digits of billing card on file

### 2FA Code Not Working
- Ensure your device's clock is synced (time-based codes are sensitive to clock skew)
- Try the next code if current one is about to expire
- Check for any time zone settings

## Browser Cache Issues
If you're unable to log in despite correct credentials:
1. Clear browser cache and cookies (Ctrl+Shift+Delete on Chrome)
2. Try an Incognito/Private browsing window
3. Try a different browser
4. Disable browser extensions temporarily

## Supported Browsers
- Google Chrome 90+
- Mozilla Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Microsoft Edge 90+

## Contact Support for Account Issues
If none of the above steps work, contact us at support@adsparkx.com with:
- Your account email
- User ID (found in Settings > Account)
- Screenshot of any error messages
- Steps already tried
