# Billing & Subscription Policy

## Subscription Plans

### Free Plan
- Up to 3 campaigns
- 500 API calls/day
- Basic analytics
- Community support only
- No credit card required

### Starter Plan — $29/month
- Up to 10 campaigns
- 5,000 API calls/day
- Standard analytics
- Email support (48hr response)
- Up to 2 team members

### Pro Plan — $99/month
- Unlimited campaigns
- 50,000 API calls/day
- Advanced analytics with export
- Priority email support (24hr)
- Up to 10 team members
- A/B testing features

### Enterprise Plan — Custom Pricing
- Unlimited everything
- Custom API limits
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantees
- SSO/SAML integration
- Custom contract terms

## Billing Cycle
- Billing occurs on the same day each month (your subscription start date)
- Annual plans receive a 20% discount
- Invoices are sent to the registered billing email

## Payment Methods
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Bank transfers (Enterprise only)
- PayPal

## Refund Policy
- Monthly plans: No refunds for partial months
- Annual plans: Pro-rated refund within 30 days of purchase
- To request a refund, email billing@adsparkx.com with your invoice number

## Upgrading/Downgrading
- Upgrades: Effective immediately; charged pro-rated for the remainder of the month
- Downgrades: Effective at the start of next billing cycle; access continues until then

## Failed Payments
1. We retry the payment 3 times over 7 days
2. You receive email notifications for each failed attempt
3. After 7 days, account is downgraded to Free plan (data is retained for 30 days)
4. Update payment details at Settings > Billing > Payment Method

## Cancellation
- Cancel anytime from Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription
- Service continues until end of current billing period
- Data is retained for 30 days post-cancellation
- To permanently delete your account and data, contact support

## Invoice & Receipt Requests
- Download invoices from Settings > Billing > Invoice History
- For custom invoice formats, contact billing@adsparkx.com

## Tax Information
- US customers: Sales tax applied where required by state law
- EU customers: VAT applied based on country; enter VAT ID for B2B exemption
- All other regions: No additional tax applied currently
