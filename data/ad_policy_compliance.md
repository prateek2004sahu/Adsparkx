# Ad Policy & Compliance Guide

## Overview
All advertisements must comply with Adsparkx's advertising policies and applicable laws. Non-compliance may result in ad rejection, account suspension, or permanent ban.

## Prohibited Content

### Absolutely Prohibited (No Exceptions)
- Child sexual abuse material
- Content facilitating human trafficking
- Products or services that harm users
- Malware, spyware, or deceptive software
- Fake news or deliberately false information designed to deceive

### Prohibited Products & Services
- Tobacco and cigarette products
- Illegal drugs and drug paraphernalia
- Counterfeit goods
- Endangered species or wildlife products
- Weapons, firearms, and ammunition (exceptions for licensed retailers with approval)
- Pyramid schemes and multi-level marketing (without pre-approval)

### Restricted Categories (Require Approval)
| Category         | Requirements                                |
|------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Alcohol          | Age-gating enabled, legal in target country |
| Gambling         | Valid gambling license submitted            |
| Pharmaceuticals  | Licensed pharmacy verification              |
| Financial services | Regulatory registration proof            |
| Political ads    | Identity verification + disclosure          |
| Healthcare       | Must not make unverified medical claims     |

## Content Standards

### Prohibited Ad Practices
- **Clickbait**: Misleading headlines that don't match landing page
- **Shock content**: Graphic violence, disturbing imagery
- **Before/after images**: For weight loss, cosmetic procedures
- **False urgency**: Fake countdown timers, fabricated scarcity
- **Hidden charges**: Any subscription that isn't clearly disclosed

### Image Guidelines
- No excessive skin exposure unless contextually appropriate
- No graphic violence or gore
- No misleading imagery (e.g., fake celebrity endorsements)
- Text overlay must be under 20% of image area
- No animated GIFs with flashing effects (seizure risk)

### Copy Guidelines
- Superlatives ("best", "#1", "world's greatest") require substantiation
- Health claims must be evidence-based and not guarantee specific results
- Testimonials must be genuine and represent typical results
- Prices shown must be accurate at time of ad display

## Landing Page Requirements
- Must be functional and load within 5 seconds
- Content must match ad creative and copy
- Must include privacy policy and terms of service
- Collect user data only with clear consent mechanisms
- Must not use auto-play video with sound
- Contact information must be visible

## Targeting Restrictions
- Cannot target users under 18 for age-restricted content
- Cannot target based on sensitive attributes (health conditions, race, religion, sexual orientation) for discriminatory purposes
- Location targeting must comply with local advertising laws

## Appeal Process for Rejected Ads

### If Your Ad Is Rejected
1. Read the rejection reason in Dashboard > Campaigns > [Ad Name] > Status
2. Review the specific policy violated (link provided in rejection notice)
3. Make required changes to the ad creative or landing page
4. Resubmit from the ad editor

### Appealing a Decision
If you believe the rejection was in error:
1. Go to Settings > Support > Ad Appeals
2. Select the rejected ad
3. Explain why you believe the ad complies with policy
4. Provide supporting documentation if applicable (licenses, certifications)
5. Appeals are reviewed within 3 business days

### Account Suspension Appeal
Email compliance@adsparkx.com with:
- Account ID
- Explanation of the issue
- Steps taken to resolve any policy violations
- Commitment to future compliance
