# Campaign Performance Optimization Guide

## Understanding Your Performance Metrics

### Key Metrics Explained
- **CPM (Cost Per Mille)**: Cost per 1,000 impressions — measures reach efficiency
- **CPC (Cost Per Click)**: Average cost for each click — measures traffic efficiency
- **CTR (Click-Through Rate)**: Clicks ÷ Impressions × 100 — measures ad relevance
- **CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)**: Total spend ÷ Conversions — measures conversion efficiency
- **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)**: Revenue ÷ Ad Spend — measures revenue efficiency
- **Frequency**: Average times each user saw your ad — high frequency = ad fatigue

## Optimization Framework

### Week 1–2: Learning Phase
- Don't make major changes during this period
- The algorithm needs 50+ conversions to optimize effectively
- Monitor delivery and ensure budget is being spent
- Set up conversion tracking before launch

### Week 3–4: Optimization Phase
Actions to take:
1. **Pause underperforming ad creatives** (CTR below 0.3% after 1,000 impressions)
2. **Scale winning creatives** (increase budget by 20% every 3 days)
3. **Refine audience** based on demographic insights
4. **Test new creatives** to prevent fatigue (frequency > 3)

### Ongoing: Scaling Phase
- Increase budget in increments of 20-30% to avoid disrupting optimization
- Introduce new audience segments (lookalike 2%, 3%)
- A/B test landing pages to improve conversion rate
- Analyze attribution model impact on reported ROAS

## Creative Optimization

### Elements to A/B Test
1. **Headline**: Test benefit-focused vs. curiosity-driven
2. **Image**: Lifestyle vs. product-only vs. user-generated content
3. **Call to Action**: "Learn More" vs. "Get Started" vs. "Shop Now"
4. **Ad Format**: Image vs. Carousel vs. Video
5. **Video Length**: 6-second vs. 15-second vs. 30-second

### Video Performance Tips
- First 3 seconds must capture attention (hook immediately)
- Add captions — 85% of users watch videos without sound
- Show product/service within first 5 seconds
- Clear CTA in last 3 seconds
- Square (1:1) video performs better on mobile than landscape

### Carousel Best Practices
- Card 1: Strongest image to hook the audience
- Cards 2-4: Feature different products or benefits
- Last card: Clear CTA with promotional offer
- Keep headline consistent across cards

## Audience Optimization

### Audience Size Guidelines
| Objective          | Recommended Audience Size |
|--------------------|--------------------------|
| Awareness          | 500K – 5M               |
| Lead Generation    | 100K – 1M               |
| Conversions        | 50K – 500K              |
| Retargeting        | 1K – 100K               |

### Lookalike Audience Strategy
- Start with 1% lookalike of best customers (highest value)
- Scale to 2% and 3% as you exhaust the 1%
- Separate awareness (3-5%) and conversion (1%) campaigns
- Refresh source audience every 30 days

### Retargeting Strategy
- 0-3 days: Warmest audience — show strong CTA
- 4-14 days: Moderately warm — show testimonials or new angle
- 15-30 days: Cooler — show awareness content or discount
- 30+ days: Exclude or use very soft touch

## Budget Optimization

### Bid Strategy Selection
| Goal                     | Recommended Strategy |
|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Maximize reach           | Lowest Cost        |
| Control CPC              | Bid Cap            |
| Control CPA              | Cost Cap           |
| Maximize ROAS            | ROAS Bidding       |

### Budget Distribution
- 70% of budget to proven campaigns
- 20% to A/B tests of existing campaigns  
- 10% to experimental new audiences/formats

## Diagnosing Poor Performance

### High Spend, Low Conversions
1. Check landing page load time (>3 seconds = high bounce rate)
2. Verify conversion pixel is firing correctly
3. Review audience quality — are the right people converting?
4. Check for offer-audience mismatch

### Low Impressions / Delivery Issues
1. Audience too narrow — expand targeting
2. Bid too low — enable Automatic bidding temporarily
3. Account spending limit reached — check billing settings
4. Ad rejected — check status in dashboard

### High CPC with Good CTR
- High competition in your audience segment
- Consider different placement options (try automatic placements)
- Test different audiences with less competition
- Improve landing page quality to increase Quality Score
