# Campaign Setup & Management Guide

## Creating Your First Campaign

### Step 1: Campaign Basics
1. Navigate to Dashboard > Campaigns > New Campaign
2. Enter a campaign name (internal reference only)
3. Select campaign objective:
   - Brand Awareness
   - Lead Generation
   - Conversions
   - Retargeting

### Step 2: Target Audience
1. Define demographics: age range, gender, location
2. Add interest categories (select up to 10)
3. Upload custom audience list (CSV with email or phone)
4. Enable Lookalike Audience to expand reach

### Step 3: Budget & Schedule
- Set daily or lifetime budget
- Minimum daily budget: $5
- Schedule start/end dates or run continuously
- Choose bid strategy: Automatic (recommended) or Manual CPC

### Step 4: Ad Creative
Supported formats:
| Format        | Dimensions     | Max File Size |
|---------------|----------------|---------------|
| Image Ad      | 1200 x 628px   | 5MB           |
| Video Ad      | 1920 x 1080px  | 500MB         |
| Carousel Ad   | 1080 x 1080px  | 5MB per card  |
| Story Ad      | 1080 x 1920px  | 30MB          |

### Step 5: Review & Launch
- Preview your ad in all placements
- Review estimated reach and CPC
- Submit for review (approval within 2-4 hours)
- Campaign goes live after approval

## Campaign Status Definitions
- **Draft**: Not submitted; still being edited
- **In Review**: Submitted, under moderation (2-4 hours)
- **Active**: Running and delivering impressions
- **Paused**: Manually stopped; can be restarted
- **Completed**: End date reached or lifetime budget depleted
- **Rejected**: Violated ad policies (reason shown in dashboard)

## Ad Rejection Reasons & Fixes
| Rejection Reason         | Fix                                      |
|--------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Misleading content       | Revise ad copy to match landing page     |
| Prohibited product       | Review Ad Policy; some categories banned |
| Poor image quality       | Re-upload at correct resolution          |
| Missing landing page URL | Add a valid destination URL              |
| Excessive text in image  | Reduce text overlay below 20%           |

## Campaign Optimization Tips
1. **A/B Test Creatives**: Run 2-3 variations to find the best performer
2. **Check Frequency**: If frequency > 5, rotate creatives to avoid fatigue
3. **Dayparting**: Schedule ads during peak engagement hours for your audience
4. **Negative Audiences**: Exclude existing customers from acquisition campaigns
5. **Conversion Tracking**: Install the Adsparkx pixel on your website for accurate attribution

## Analytics & Reporting
- Real-time reporting available in Dashboard > Analytics
- Key metrics: Impressions, Clicks, CTR, CPC, Conversions, ROAS
- Export reports as CSV or PDF from Analytics > Export
- Schedule automated weekly/monthly reports via Analytics > Scheduled Reports

## Common Campaign Issues

### Low Impressions
- Check if campaign is approved and active
- Verify audience size is not too narrow (minimum 10,000 recommended)
- Increase bid or daily budget
- Ensure ad creative meets specifications

### Low CTR (below 0.5%)
- Refresh ad creative — try different images/copy
- Refine audience targeting
- Test different call-to-action buttons

### High CPC
- Narrow audience targeting to more relevant segments
- Improve ad relevance score
- Increase quality score by improving landing page experience
