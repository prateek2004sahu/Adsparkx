# Service Level Agreement (SLA) & Uptime Policy

## Uptime Commitment

| Plan       | Uptime SLA | Monthly Downtime Allowance |
|------------|------------|---------------------------|
| Free       | No SLA     | N/A                       |
| Starter    | 99.5%      | ~3.6 hours/month          |
| Pro        | 99.9%      | ~43 minutes/month         |
| Enterprise | 99.99%     | ~4.3 minutes/month        |

## Downtime Definition
"Downtime" means the platform is completely inaccessible or error rate exceeds 50% for more than 5 consecutive minutes.

Excluded from downtime calculation:
- Scheduled maintenance (announced 48 hours in advance)
- Issues caused by customer's infrastructure or third-party services
- Force majeure events

## Scheduled Maintenance
- Typically performed Tuesdays, 2:00–4:00 AM UTC
- Announced via status.adsparkx.com and email at least 48 hours prior
- Emergency maintenance may be performed with less notice for critical security patches

## Service Credits
If SLA is breached, customers receive service credits:

| Uptime Achieved | Credit (% of monthly fee) |
|-----------------|---------------------------|
| 99.0% – 99.49%  | 10%                       |
| 95.0% – 98.99%  | 25%                       |
| Below 95%       | 50%                       |

### How to Claim Credits
1. Submit claim within 30 days of the incident
2. Email sla@adsparkx.com with:
   - Account ID
   - Date and duration of incident
   - Impact description
3. Credits applied to next invoice (not refunded as cash)

## Support Response Time SLA

| Priority    | Description                              | First Response  | Resolution Target |
|-------------|------------------------------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| P1 Critical | Platform down, data loss risk            | 1 hour          | 4 hours           |
| P2 High     | Major feature broken, no workaround      | 4 hours         | 24 hours          |
| P3 Medium   | Feature impaired, workaround available   | 8 hours         | 72 hours          |
| P4 Low      | General questions, minor issues          | 24 hours        | 7 days            |

*Response times are during business hours (9 AM – 6 PM IST, Mon–Fri) for Starter/Pro. Enterprise customers get 24/7 P1/P2 coverage.*

## Incident Communication
- All incidents posted at status.adsparkx.com in real time
- Subscribe to email/SMS updates on the status page
- Post-incident reports published within 72 hours of resolution for P1/P2 incidents

## Business Impact Escalation
For incidents with significant business impact (revenue loss, customer-facing outages):
- Email escalations@adsparkx.com with "BUSINESS IMPACT" in subject
- Include: estimated impact ($), affected campaigns, timeline
- Enterprise customers: call dedicated account manager directly

## Current System Status
Visit status.adsparkx.com to view:
- Real-time component status
- Active incident updates
- Historical uptime data (past 90 days)
- Upcoming maintenance schedule
